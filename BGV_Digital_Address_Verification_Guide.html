How Our Verification Process Works

This is the exact candidate self-verification flow we need the vendor to support — from case creation in our system to the final result returned to our application.

🗂️

Case Created in Our Application

Our system generates a unique Case ID and the operator fills in the candidate details — this is the data we will send to the vendor API.

Case ID (our own) Candidate Name Email ID Permanent Address Current Address
🔌

API Call to Vendor with Our Case ID

Our application fires an API request to the vendor, passing all the above fields. Critically, our own Case ID is sent as the reference_id or client_ref_id so results map back automatically.

reference_id = Our Case ID Both addresses supported
📧

Vendor Sends Email to Candidate

The vendor's system automatically sends an email to the candidate containing a unique verification link and OTP in the same email. No action needed from our ops team.

Link in email OTP in email Pre-filled address details
📱

Candidate Completes Verification on Mobile

The candidate opens the link on their phone. On iPhone → web app in Safari (no app download). On Android → native app. They enter the OTP, take a live photo of their house, and GPS is captured automatically on submit.

iPhone = Web App (no download) Android = Native App OTP entry House photo (live) GPS auto-captured
🔁

Result Returned to Our System

Once complete, the vendor fires a webhook to our callback URL. The payload includes our original Case ID, GPS coordinates, house photo, and verification status — automatically mapped in our application.

Our Case ID in payload GPS coordinates House photo URL Webhook / callback

10 Questions to Ask Every Vendor

Ask these in your demo before signing any contract. Get written confirmation on all of them.

01

Does your candidate verification link work as a web app on iPhone Safari without requiring any app download?

02

When I pass my own Case ID as reference_id, does that exact ID come back in your webhook payload when the case closes?

03

For dual-address verification (permanent + current), does the candidate get one link to complete both, or two separate links?

04

Is GPS capture mandatory? What happens if the candidate's location is turned off on their device?

05

Are you UIDAI-authorised directly or through a licensed ASA/AUA? Provide your UIDAI certificate.

06

What is your SLA for TAT on digital address verification for candidates in Tamil Nadu?

07

Do you have a BGV company / partner pricing tier? What is the minimum monthly volume commitment?

08

Can we run a pilot batch of 20–50 cases before committing to a formal contract?

09

How long is candidate data stored, and how is it protected under the DPDP Act 2023?

10

What is your uptime SLA? Share last 12 months' availability data for the address verification API.
